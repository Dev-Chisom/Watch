Braun Classic Watch

The Braun watch is a reissue of the original 1970's design from
renowned design team Dietrich Lubs and Dieter Rams, both of
whom have work featured in the Museum's collection. The large
watch features a numbered face and the smaller watch has
index lines. Made of a matte stainless-steel case, black dail,
mineral glass a three-hand quartz movement, and a leather
band, Water-resistant.
